About Me

Hello, I am Nicole Lindsoe! I am a UX/UI designer, as well as a graphic designer. My other skills include illustration and social media marketing.

I specialize in prototyping and graphic design, and enjoy solving everyday user problems to provide effortless user experiences. I am proficient in Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign, Figma, Miro, Invision.

I grew in Shoreview, Minnesota and currently live in Afton. I’m looking to relocate eventually, but I’m open to where I go.

I’m an easy-going, adventurous person with a passion for art and design, animals, nature, and travelling. When I’m not working on design, you can find me riding my horses, painting, hiking, and travelling. I’m a major animal lover and my family has two dogs, three cats, and four horses on our hobby farm.
